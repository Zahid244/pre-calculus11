Pre-Calculus 11: Quadratics

Welcome!

Hello and welcome to my Pre-Calculus 11 project website! My name is Zahid Khamoosh, and I created this site to help students like you better understand one of the most important units in the course: Quadratics.

Quadratic functions, equations, and graphs appear frequently in both math class and real life — from calculating the height of objects in physics to understanding profit in business. However, many students struggle with this topic because it combines algebra, graphing, and formulas all in one. I built this site with the goal of making these ideas simpler, clearer, and more interactive.

  • Quadratic Functions: Learn what they are and how they work
  • Quadratic Equations: Explore different ways to solve them
  • Quadratic Graphs: Understand how to sketch and analyze them visually

Everything here is explained step by step, with examples, visuals, and links to interactive tools like Desmos to help you see how quadratics behave in action. Whether you're reviewing for a test or just want a clearer explanation, I hope this resource helps make Pre-Calculus feel a little less stressful and a lot more doable.
